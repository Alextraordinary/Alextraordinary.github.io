First Post

When I started this website about a month ago, I wasn’t sure exactly what it would look like or what kind of content I wanted to include. Thankfully, my older brother who’s an experienced computer programmer pointed me in the right direction and helped me create my website. That being said, I decided that one thing I wanted to have was a blog.

I’m starting this blog because I feel like it would be a good way for me to put my thoughts out there and say everything that’s on my mind. I guess this is like my personal diary, except it’s not really a diary since I’m publicizing it. This won’t be a very research or news-oriented blog, nor will it be really formal or professional; I’ll simply be writing about whatever’s on my mind.

With all of that out of the way, I thought it would be nice to introduce myself a bit more
